Thai Lime and Chili Cashews

Inspired by the iconic Tom Yum Soup, these cashews feature a blend of Thai lime, chili, and lemongrass flavors!

Ingredients

Ingredient Details
Cashews The main ingredient in the snack
Thai Lime Leaves The cashews are covered in dried or fried makrut lime leaves
Chili The cashews are spicy and have a lot of chili
Rich Bran Oil The cashews are coated in their own oils when toasted
Tom Yum Seasoning Contains maltodextrin, sugar, salt, yeast extract powder, citric acid, garlic powder, lemongrass, silicon dioxide, chili powder, white pepper, natural lemon flavor, and paprika oleoresin
