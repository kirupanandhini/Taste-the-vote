Matcha Cookies

Matcha cookies are soft, chewy cookies with a distinct, vibrant green color, infused with a noticeable matcha flavor, offering a subtle earthy taste and a light caffeine kick!

Ingredients

Ingredient Details
Brown Sugar Adds moisture and flavor depth
White Sugar Helps maintain the cookies' green color
Matcha Green Tea Powder Made from finely ground young green tea leaves
Melted Coconut Oil For richness
Natural Flavors Enhances the cookies' taste experience

Calories: 160 per serving | Fat: Medium | Gluten-Free: No
