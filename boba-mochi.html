Boba Mochi

Boba Mochi is a delicious treat featuring soft, chewy mochi filled with a creamy boba-infused center. Perfect for a sweet snack or dessert!

Ingredients

Ingredient Details
Glutinous Rice Flour Main component of the soft mochi exterior
Tapioca Pearls Provides the chewy boba texture
Sugar Sweetens the mochi and boba filling
Coconut Milk Adds creaminess to the filling
Natural Flavors Enhances the boba taste experience

Nutritional Info

Calories: 120 per serving | Fat: Low | Gluten-Free: Yes
