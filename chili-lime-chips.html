Chili Lime Chips

Chili Lime Chips are crunchy, rolled corn tortilla chips coated with a zesty chili and lime seasoning!

Ingredients

Ingredient Details
Stone ground yellow corn The base of the chips
Vegetable Oil High-oleic sunflower, safflower, or canola oil
Paprika Extract A natural color additive
Silicon Dioxide An anti-caking agent
Hot Chili and Lime Seasoning Contains salt, yeast, cornstarch, cane sugar, citric acid, yeast extract, corn maltodextrin, gum arabic, malic acid, lime juice concentrate, onion powder, and more

Calories: 150 per serving | Fat: Moderate | Gluten-Free: Yes
