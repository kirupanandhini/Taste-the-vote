Peanut Butter Cups

Peanut Butter Cups are made with peanut butter from slow-roasted Virginia peanuts and rich dark chocolate from high-quality cacao beans!

Ingredients

Ingredient Details
Peanut Butter Made from slow-roasted, ground Virginia peanuts
Milk Chocolate Contains cocoa butter, chocolate liquor, sugar, milkfat, soy lecithin, and natural flavor
Palm Kernel Oil Used in the chocolate coating
Lactose Found in the milk chocolate
Salt A flavoring ingredient
